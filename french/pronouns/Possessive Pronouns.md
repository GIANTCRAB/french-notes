Because words like my and your change based on the gender of the word after them!

# Singulier

| French | gender | English           |
| ------ | ------ | ----------------- |
| ton    | male   | your              |
| ta     | female | your              |
| mon    | male   | mine              |
| ma     | female | mine              |
| notre  |        | our               |
| votre  |        | your (vous)       |
| leur   |        | their (ils/elles) |
